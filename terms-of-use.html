2. Description of Service

The App is a scientific utility tool designed for laboratory professionals. It provides:

  • Laboratory calculators (dilutions, concentrations, cell counting, molarity, transfection, virus titer, and more)
  • Cell culture tracking with confluency prediction and growth curves
  • Experiment tracker with checklists, notes, photos, and linked items
  • Plate layout planner for 6 to 96-well plates
  • Protocol library with step-by-step timers and built-in templates
  • Digital lab notebook with procedures, quick notes, voice-to-text dictation (on-device), and export to PDF, CSV, or text
  • Inventory management for cell lines, reagents, virus stocks, frozen vials, and equipment
  • Lab reference data (molecular weights, buffer recipes, fluorophore spectra, GHS safety symbols, and more)
  • Apple Watch companion app for timer control
  • Today dashboard with tasks, calendar, and lab alerts
  • CSV backup and import for data portability

The App is intended as a productivity aid for use alongside standard laboratory practices.
