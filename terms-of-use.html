2. Intended Purpose and Scope of Use

The App is a productivity and reference tool for laboratory research, providing:

  • Laboratory calculators (dilutions, concentrations, cell counting, molarity, transfection, virus titer, and others).
  • Cell culture tracking with confluency estimation, growth prediction, and per-culture medium-change schedules.
  • Experiment tracker with checklists, notes, photos, and linked items. Save any experiment as a reusable template; export any experiment as an A4 PDF report.
  • Plate layout planner for 6 to 96-well plates with up to four colour-coded conditions, per-well notes, PNG/PDF export, and up to 50 steps of undo/redo.
  • Protocol library with step-by-step timers and built-in templates.
  • Digital lab notebook with procedures, quick notes, voice-to-text dictation (on-device), and measurement tables. Notebook exports to CSV.
  • Inventory management for cell lines, reagents (including multiple per-reagent lots), virus stocks, frozen vials, and equipment. Freezer inventory includes a box-map grid view.
  • Lab reference data (molecular weights, buffer recipes, fluorophore spectra, GHS safety symbols).
  • Apple Watch companion app for timer control and a Today's Tasks tab with tap-to-complete sync, plus a "Lab Timer" watch-face complication.
  • Home Screen and Lock Screen widgets, Live Activities with action buttons, and (on iOS 18) a Control Center "Lab Timer" button.
  • Today dashboard with tasks, calendar, lab alerts, and experiment-step due dates.
  • CSV export and import for data portability.
