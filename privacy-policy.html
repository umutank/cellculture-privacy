Privacy Policy

Last updated: 06/04/2026

Overview

Cell Culture and Lab Assistant ("the App") is developed by Umut Tank. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we handle your data.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data. The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information, usage data, or analytics.

Data Storage

All data you enter in the App (cultures, experiments, calculations, timers, cell line information, reagents, plate layouts, notebook entries) is stored locally on your device. If iCloud is enabled, data is also backed up to your personal iCloud account (see iCloud section below). No data is sent to any third-party server or service.

Apple Watch

If you use the companion Apple Watch app, timer data is transferred between your iPhone and Apple Watch using Apple's WatchConnectivity framework. This data stays on your devices and is not sent to any server. Timer state is cached on the Apple Watch so timers display correctly between sessions; this cached data is limited to timer names and durations.

iCloud Backup

If you have iCloud enabled on your device, your app data is automatically backed up to your personal iCloud account when changes are made. This data is stored in your private iCloud container and the developer has no access to it. You can manage or delete this data in Settings > [your name] > iCloud > Manage Account Storage.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Camera and Photos

The App may request access to your camera to take photos of experiments, gels, and plates for your lab notebook. Photos are stored locally on your device and backed up to iCloud if enabled. Photos are never sent to any server or third party.

Siri and Shortcuts

The App provides Siri integration for starting lab timers. Timer names and durations provided via Siri are processed by Apple according to Apple's privacy policy. No data is sent to the developer through Siri.

Search and Widgets

The App indexes your items (culture names, experiment titles, reagent names, etc.) in the system search index (Spotlight) so you can find them from your home screen. The App also shares limited data (timer status, task counts) with its home screen widgets. All of this data remains on your device and is not transmitted externally.

Internet Access

The App does not require an internet connection and does not make any app-initiated network requests. iCloud backup synchronization and App Store review prompts are handled by the operating system.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

Data Retention

All app data is stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all local data. If iCloud backup was enabled, your data remains in your personal iCloud storage until you manually delete it from Settings > [your name] > iCloud > Manage Storage. The developer has no ability to access, retain, or recover any of your data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
