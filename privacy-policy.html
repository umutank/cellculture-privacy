Privacy Policy

Last updated: 11/04/2026

Overview

Cell Culture and Lab Assistant ("the App") is developed by Umut Tank. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we handle your data.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data. The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information, usage data, or analytics.

Data Storage

All data you enter in the App (cultures, experiments, calculations, timers, cell line information, reagents, virus stocks, frozen vial inventory, lab equipment, equipment maintenance records, plate layouts, notebook entries) is stored locally on your device. If iCloud Backup is enabled, data is also included in your standard iCloud device backup (see iCloud Backup section below). No data is sent to any third-party server or service.

Apple Watch

If you use the companion Apple Watch app, timer data is transferred between your iPhone and Apple Watch using Apple's WatchConnectivity framework. This data stays on your devices and is not sent to any server. Timer state is cached on the Apple Watch so timers display correctly between sessions; this cached data is limited to timer names and durations.

iCloud Backup

All app data is stored locally on your device. If you have iCloud Backup enabled in your iOS Settings, your app data is included in your standard iCloud device backup alongside other app data on your device. There is no active cross-device sync; your data does not transfer automatically between devices. The developer has no access to your iCloud backups. You can manage iCloud Backup in Settings > [your name] > iCloud > iCloud Backup.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Camera and Photos

The App may request access to your camera to take photos of experiments, gels, and plates for your lab notebook. Photos are stored locally on your device and backed up to iCloud if enabled. Photos are never sent to any server or third party.

Microphone and Speech Recognition

The App offers a voice-to-text feature that lets you dictate lab notes hands-free (for example, while wearing gloves at the bench). When you tap the Voice Note button, the App accesses your device's microphone and uses Apple's on-device speech recognition to convert your speech into text.

All speech processing happens entirely on your device. No audio recordings or transcriptions are sent to any server, the developer, or any third party. The App uses Apple's SFSpeechRecognizer with on-device recognition enforced. Audio buffers are processed in real-time and discarded immediately after transcription. No audio is stored, saved, or retained by the App.

The App will request your permission to access the microphone and speech recognition the first time you use the voice feature. You can revoke these permissions at any time in Settings > Cell Culture and Lab Assistant. The voice feature is entirely optional; the App works fully without it.

Siri and Shortcuts

The App provides Siri Shortcuts integration, allowing you to start lab timers using voice commands or the Shortcuts app. Timer names, durations, and completion status may be shared with Siri to enable this functionality. No data is sent to external servers. All Siri data is processed by Apple according to Apple's privacy policy.

Search and Widgets

The App indexes your items (culture names, experiment titles, reagent names, etc.) in the system search index (Spotlight) so you can find them from your home screen. The App also shares limited data (timer status, task counts) with its home screen widgets. All of this data remains on your device and is not transmitted externally.

Internet Access

The App works fully offline. iCloud Backup and App Store review prompts are handled by the operating system and only occur when an internet connection is available. The App does not make any network requests to the developer's servers or any third party.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

Data Retention

All app data is stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all local data. If iCloud backup was enabled, your data remains in your personal iCloud storage until you manually delete it from Settings > [your name] > iCloud > Manage Storage. The developer has no ability to access, retain, or recover any of your data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
